Shops, Neopoints, and a Kid-Sized Economy

Neopets introduced millions of young players to a simplified version of an online economy. Players earned Neopoints from games, ran their own shops, watched prices on the Shop Wizard, and tried to grab rare items before anyone else. Even though the currency was fictional, the behaviors felt very real: saving, spending, hoarding, and sometimes regretting a terrible trade.
